What I did

  1. Randomly draw 1000 articles from each country
  2. Remove duplications
  3. Obtain word embeddings
  4. Clustering

Word Embeddings

Assumption:
DFM contains three types of information

  1. Systematic language variations
  2. Topical variations
  3. Non-topical variations (eg random noise)

Singular Vector Decomposition

  1. Rank dimentions by the variance they explain
  2. Discard the highest ones
    (ie they are language variations)
  3. Discard the lowest ones
    (ie they are noise)

K-Medoids Clustering

  1. Calculate document-to-document similarity
  2. Cluster all documents into 13 clusters
    (each cluster represents one topic)
  3. Extract the most central documents of each cluster for validation
